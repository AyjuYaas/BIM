Nepal is a beautiful country located in South Asia, bordered by China to the north and India to the south, east, and west. It is home to the world's highest peak, Mount Everest, as well as other stunning mountain ranges, lakes, rivers, and diverse wildlife. If you're planning to visit Nepal in 2023, here are some things you may want to consider: Best time to visit: The best time to visit Nepal is during the autumn season (September-November) and spring season (March-May) when the weather is mild, and the skies are clear. These seasons are perfect for trekking, sightseeing, and outdoor activities. Visa requirements: Visitors to Nepal must obtain a visa before arrival. You can either apply for a visa online or get one on arrival at the airport. You will need to provide a passport with at least six months' validity and a recent passport-sized photo.
